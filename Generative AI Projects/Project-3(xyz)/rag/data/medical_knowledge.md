# Medical Knowledge Base

## Pneumonia

Pneumonia is an infection that inflames air sacs in one or both lungs. Common symptoms include fever, cough, chest pain, fatigue, and shortness of breath. X-ray findings may include infiltrates or consolidation.

## Influenza

Influenza is a contagious respiratory illness. Symptoms often include fever, cough, sore throat, body aches, headache, and fatigue. Antiviral treatment may be considered early for high-risk patients.

## Common Cold

The common cold is usually caused by respiratory viruses. Symptoms commonly include runny nose, sore throat, cough, sneezing, and mild headache. Supportive care is typical.

## Migraine

Migraine is a neurological condition often causing severe headache, nausea, light sensitivity, and functional impairment. Red flags such as sudden worst headache or neurological deficits require urgent evaluation.

## Gastritis

Gastritis is inflammation of the stomach lining. Symptoms may include nausea, upper abdominal discomfort, vomiting, and appetite changes.

## Clinical Safety

AI decision support should summarize evidence, surface possible risk, and recommend clinical review. It should not replace licensed clinician judgment.
